The Best Movies According to Andrew

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Best In Show

A movie my wife and I have loved since the first time we saw it, and we still watch it at least once yearly. It's a mockumentary by director Christopher Guest that centers on show dogs and the wacky people who own and obsess over them.

Akira

A beautifully animated and psychologically mindbending anime from the 90s.

Igby Goes Down

A coming of age film starring Kieran Culkin and his navigation through life with his dysfunctional family. Often compared to the book The Catcher In The Rye.
